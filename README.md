## About

Adan G. Puffelis is a filmmaker, educator, and creative technologist based in Los Angeles. With an MFA in Cinema from San Francisco State University and a career spanning documentary, lifestyle, and commercial production, he brings both a director's vision and a producer's discipline to every project.

Adan has built and managed end-to-end production workflows from on-set acquisition through post-production delivery and applies that systems thinking to Digital Asset Management, pipeline design, and creative operations. He is equally at home behind the camera and behind the curriculum, having taught film production and visual storytelling at the university level.

He is currently seeking opportunities in creative technology and DAM, documentary and lifestyle directing and producing, and film education where craft, process, and purpose intersect.
